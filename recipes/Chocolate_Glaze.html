The Best Chocolate Glaze

Description

This chocolate glaze is a glossy, translucent coating of chocolate applied to the outer surface of a dish by dipping, dripping, or using a brush. Depending on its nature and intended effect, a glaze may be applied before or after cooking.

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons hot water, divided

Steps

  1. Melt cocoa and butter together in a small saucepan over low heat, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  2. Stir in confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract. Stir in hot water, 1 teaspoon at a time, until glaze is thick and smooth.
